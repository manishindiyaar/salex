# 1. Goals and Background Context

## Goals

The primary goals for the V1 launch of Salex are:
* To solve the core operational challenges of queue and appointment chaos for small to medium-sized Indian salons.
* To provide a frictionless, app-less, and intuitive booking experience for the end customer, completed in three clicks or less via WhatsApp.
* To empower salon owners with a simple, reliable tool to manage their business, reduce missed revenue, and gain basic insights without needing extensive training.
* To build a robust, business-agnostic backend engine that can be scaled to other SMB verticals in the future, fulfilling the "Build Once, Scale Everywhere" principle.
* To achieve a notification delivery success rate greater than 99.5%, ensuring merchants never miss a booking.

## Background Context

Local salons in India represent a high-value service industry but are often hampered by operational inefficiency. Salon owners, like our persona Rajesh, manage a constant, chaotic mix of walk-in customers and phone-call appointments. This leads to long, unpredictable waiting times for customers and significant missed revenue for owners during peak hours. Existing software solutions are often too complex, expensive, or require business documentation that our target merchants do not possess. Direct feedback from salon owners confirms that these solutions are often perceived as prohibitively expensive and complex, reinforcing the need for a simpler, more accessible tool.

For end customers, like our persona Anjali, the process is fraught with friction. They face uncertain waits of up to 90 minutes and the inconvenience of phone calls that often go unanswered. Salex addresses these parallel pain points by leveraging the ubiquity of WhatsApp to create a simple, reliable "Operating System" for these businesses, starting with an appointment and queue management system.
