# 5. Epic List

Here is the proposed high-level roadmap for the V1 development of **Salex**.

* **Epic 1: Foundation & Merchant Onboarding**
    * **Goal:** To establish the core technical foundation and allow a salon owner to sign up, create their business profile, and fully configure their services and hours.

* **Epic 2: The Core Booking Loop**
    * **Goal:** To enable the complete, end-to-end booking experience, allowing a customer to book an appointment via WhatsApp and the merchant to receive a reliable notification and manage it on their dashboard.

* **Epic 3: V1 Enhancements & Intelligence**
    * **Goal:** To enhance the core product with intelligent features for returning customers, a streamlined "Duty Mode" for walk-ins, and basic daily business insights.

## Rationale

This epic structure is designed for a logical, incremental rollout of value:

1.  **Epic 1 (Onboarding)** must come first. Without a solid foundation and a way for merchants to get their business onto the platform, no other feature matters. Completing this epic means we have a platform ready for users, even if they can't take bookings yet.
2.  **Epic 2 (Booking Loop)** delivers the central promise of the product. Once this epic is complete, the system is fully functional and can be shipped to our first users to generate immediate value.
3.  **Epic 3 (Enhancements)** builds upon that core loop. These features make the product smarter and more efficient, but they depend on the core booking system already being in place.

This sequence ensures that we build the most critical features first and can, in theory, deploy a valuable increment of the product after each epic is completed.
